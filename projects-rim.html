Here, I will provide you with an overview
of my project for my class Research in Management.

Summary

Analysis whether divestiture experience shortens divestiture timeline.

Problem Statement

Many companies strive to become leaner by divesting parts of the company. Those divestiture processes are often costly and time-intensive. Therefore, companies strive to minimize the related effort while keeping the process quality. One factor in the effort is the time needed to pursue a divestiture. In this project, I analyzed whether previous divestiture experience can help a company to speed up the divestiture process.

Solution Approach

The following steps were followed to derive sound recommendations for companies that pursue divestiture strategies:

Study design: Various linear regressions leveraging a dataset that includes acquitition data from multiple countries and industries.

Data cleaning: Several colums in the dataset had to be cleaned since some variables such as deal value were stored in different formats across the data set. Moreover, some data was missing for a subset of the included deals. These deals were excluded from the analysis.

Data intuition: To better understand the data set, several analyses were run in STATA, Tableau, and Excel to obtain summary statistics, visual representations, and further insights.

Model building: Various regression models were built such as zero-truncated (the recommended method for the task on hand) and zero-inflated negative binomial regression models. Furthermore, some models were built that included only specific industries or geographies to compare results cross-industry and cross-culture. The moderating variables such as deal size, cultural complexity, and organizational slack were included.

Results presentation: The final results were presented to the class and professors through a PowerPoint presentation.

Outcome

There is no significant indication that divestiture experience speeds up the divestiture process. Out of the moderating variables, only deal size and payment method showed significant relationships with divestiture speed.

Tools Used

STATA, Tableau, Excel, PowerPoint.
